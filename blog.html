My country India

Geography: India is the seventh-largest country in the world by land area and the second-most populous country, after China. It shares borders with Pakistan to the northwest, China and Nepal to the north, Bhutan to the northeast, and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. To the south, it is bounded by the Indian Ocean.

Capital and Major Cities: The capital city of India is New Delhi, which is a part of the larger National Capital Territory of Delhi. Other major cities include Mumbai (formerly Bombay), Kolkata (formerly Calcutta), Chennai (formerly Madras), and Bangalore.

Culture: India has a rich cultural heritage that dates back thousands of years. It is known for its diverse traditions, languages, religions, and cuisines. Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, Buddhism, and Jainism are some of the major religions practiced in the country.

Languages: India is a linguistically diverse nation with several languages spoken across its regions. Hindi and English are the official languages, but there are also 21 officially recognized regional languages.
